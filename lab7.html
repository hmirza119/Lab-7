Lebron James

The King of Basketball

Lebron James is widely considered one of the greatest basketball players of all time. Known for his incredible skills and talents that he brings to the court, leadership, and longevity, he has become an inspiration to the youth. Beyond the court, Lebron is a dedicated family man and a passionate advocate for education and social justice

Early Life

Lebron James was born on December 30, 1984 in Akron Ohio to a 16 year old single mother. He played both Basketball and Football in high school leading the basketball team to 3 state championships. He was awarded Ohio's Mr. Basketball (high-school player of the year) three times. In his senior year of high school, he was named the national high-school player of the year.
He was drafted 1st pick in the 2003 NBA Draft by the Cleveland Cavaliers

Achievements

  • Four time NBA Champion (2012, 2013, 2016, 2020)
  • Four time NBA Finals MVP (2012, 2013, 2016, 2020)
  • Four time NBA MVP (2009, 2010, 2012, 2013)
  • 21 time NBA all star (2005-2024)
  • 3 time NBA all star game MVP (2006, 2008, 2018)
  • 13 time all NBA team selections (2006, 2008-2018, 2020)
  • 5 time all NBA first team selections
  • 1 time NBA Scoring Leader (2008)
  • 1 time NBA Assists Leader (2020)
  • 4 time olympic gold medalists
